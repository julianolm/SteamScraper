Control one of the few humans left in the world in an epic battle against the Cyber Monkeys. Discover secrets and do quests until you achieve the longed-for freedom. Climb to the top of the building, facing hordes of robotic apes and other beasts, uncovering ancient secrets linked to your past.

FIGHT TO TAKE CONTROL OF THE CITY

As a human and his mysterious shark-looking weapon, you'll use several different shots to face the Cyber Monkeys, while discovering more about the story with each attempt.

FRANTIC COMBAT

Face dozens of enemies! Cybernetic apes, robots, androids, and genetically modified animals. Defeat them with your skills!

GREAT ARSENAL

Your weapon is capable of storing different shots. You will have to choose wisely which is the best shot for each occasion.

RECOGABILITY

New surprises await you with each failed attempt. You will be able to use various resources to improve and become stronger and stronger.

A MYSTERIOUS WORLD

During your journey, new characters may emerge and each one will tell their vision of what may be happening in the world of Neon City. Interact with NPCs and discover precious information.
